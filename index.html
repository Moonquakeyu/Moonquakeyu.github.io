Evidence is the interface where models meet the physical and clinical world.

Hi, I am Peiang (Ignatius) Miao, an MSc student in Metaverse Technology at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My research centers on evidence-grounded multimodal AI, with current work spanning industrial visual inspection, clinical-dental LLM agents, spatial transcriptomics, and auditable affective computing.

I am especially interested in AI systems that do more than predict a label: they should expose component evidence, retrieve supporting knowledge, reason under uncertainty, and produce outputs that human experts can inspect. This theme connects my work on wafer defect recognition, implant-dentistry protocol agents, spatial/single-cell analysis, and vision-language reporting.

Selected Projects

  • EviComp / CrystalComp - compositional wafer defect recognition with component evidence masks, counterfactual benchmark design, and calibrated target-domain adaptation.
  • CADENCE - clinical-dental LLM agent for implant-research protocol generation, literature-backed critique, and expert workflow support.
  • SpaGVAE - structured spatial-prior graph autoencoder for robust spatial domain identification in spatial transcriptomics.
  • Bio-CPD - entropy-weighted divergence framework for single-cell critical transition detection along pseudotime trajectories.
  • SS-VLM / EmoSense - auditable affective computing systems with spectral-spatial evidence, retrieval grounding, and constrained report generation.
